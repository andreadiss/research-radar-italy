# Product Spec

## Positioning

Research Radar Italy is an academic jobs board for Italy. The MVP should do what Academic Jobs Italy does, but with a better user experience, better classification, better alerts, and a path toward wider source coverage.

## Primary Audience

- PhD candidates looking for funded doctoral calls
- Postdocs and research fellows
- Assegnisti and contrattisti
- Early-career researchers seeking RTT or research institute roles
- International candidates evaluating opportunities in Italy

## MVP Promise

Users can find relevant Italian academic and research opportunities in less than two minutes, understand the key details without opening several PDFs, and create alerts for new matching calls.

## Scope

### In Scope

- MUR/Cineca calls as the primary data source
- All disciplines
- PhD, assegni, postdoc, contratti di ricerca, tecnologi, RTT, professor calls
- PRIN and PNRR tagging when detectable
- Search and filters
- Detail page with normalized fields
- Saved searches and alerts in the near-term roadmap

### Out of Scope for First Clickable Prototype

- Real authentication
- Production database
- Payment or sponsored listings
- Full AI extraction
- Full source coverage beyond MUR/Cineca

## User Journeys

1. A postdoc searches for "machine learning" and filters by region and funding.
2. A PhD candidate filters for doctoral calls and deadlines in the next 60 days.
3. A researcher opens a detail page and quickly checks deadline, institution, sector, duration, amount, requirements, and official link.
4. A returning user saves a search and receives new matches by email.

## Success Metrics

- Imported MUR/Cineca calls per day
- Median time from source publication to site availability
- Search-to-click conversion
- Newsletter signups
- Saved alerts
- Weekly returning users
- Classification accuracy
